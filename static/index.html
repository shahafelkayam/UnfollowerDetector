How to download your Instagram data

1

Open Meta Accounts Center

Go directly to Your Information and Permissions on Instagram.

2

Request your data

Click "Download your information", then select "Some of your information".

3

Select Followers and Following

Under the Connections section, check only "Followers and following".

4

Choose JSON format

Set the format to JSON (not HTML) and date range to All time. Then submit the request.

5

Download & upload here

You'll receive an email within 5-30 minutes. Download the ZIP file and upload it directly here — no need to extract.
